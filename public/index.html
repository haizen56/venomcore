About Us

Born from Passion, Built for Legacy

Venom Core was founded by a team of obsessive game-makers who believed that interactive storytelling could rival any other art form. Seven years later, we're proving it every day.

From our studio in the Pacific Northwest, we combine the rigour of AAA production with the creative freedom of independent development. Every game we ship is a statement — that craft matters, stories matter, and players deserve better.
